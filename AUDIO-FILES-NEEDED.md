This project needs three audio files to be placed in the `public` folder. These files are **not included** in the repository due to copyright considerations.

## Required Files

Place these files in the `public/` folder:

### 1. christmas-music.mp3
- **Purpose**: Background Christmas music
- **Requirements**:
  - Format: MP3
  - Duration: 2-5 minutes (loopable)
  - Quality: Good audio quality
  - Volume: Will be set to 30% automatically

### 2. bell-sound.mp3
- **Purpose**: Bell sound effect
- **Requirements**:
  - Format: MP3
  - Duration: 1-3 seconds
  - Type: Single bell ring or jingle
  - Volume: Will be set to 40% automatically

### 3. chime-sound.mp3
- **Purpose**: Chime sound effect
- **Requirements**:
  - Format: MP3
  - Duration: 1-3 seconds
  - Type: Pleasant chime or bell sound
  - Volume: Will be set to 40% automatically

## Where to Find Royalty-Free Audio

### Free Sources:
1. **Pixabay Music** - https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Search for "christmas music", "bells", "chimes"
   - 100% free, no attribution required

2. **Free Music Archive** - https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Filter by "Christmas" genre
   - Check license requirements

3. **Freesound** - https://freesound.org/
   - Great for sound effects
   - Search for "bell" and "chime"
   - Most require attribution

4. **YouTube Audio Library** - https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary
   - Free music and sound effects
   - Filter by "No attribution required"

5. **Incompetech** - https://incompetech.com/music/
   - Royalty-free music
   - Attribution required

### Recommended Searches:
- "christmas music instrumental"
- "jingle bells sound effect"
- "wind chimes"
- "sleigh bells"
- "church bells"

## Installation Steps

1. Download your chosen audio files
2. Rename them exactly as:
   - `christmas-music.mp3`
   - `bell-sound.mp3`
   - `chime-sound.mp3`
3. Place them in the `public/` folder
4. The app will automatically load them

## Alternative: Use Your Own Music

You can use any MP3 files you own! Just make sure to:
- Name them correctly
- Place them in the `public/` folder
- Ensure they're in MP3 format

## Testing Without Audio

The app will work without audio files, but:
- Music player will show errors in console
- Sound effects won't play
- All other features will work normally
